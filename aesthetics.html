Aesthetic Services

Medical-grade skin, hair and laser treatments by a qualified gynaecologist — safe, evidence-based, and tailored to your skin type.

In this page Skin Treatments Hair Treatments Laser Procedures

Skin Treatments

What is it?

Medical-grade facials, chemical peels, and targeted treatments address pigmentation, acne, and uneven skin tone using clinically proven active ingredients — customised to Indian skin tones and sensitivities.

Who needs it?

Melasma & Pigmentation Acne & Post-Acne Marks Uneven Skin Tone Age Spots & Sun Damage Dull & Dehydrated Skin Open Pores

What to expect

  1. 1
    Skin Consultation & Analysis

    Detailed skin-type assessment; medical history review; treatment goals defined.

  2. 2
    Personalised Protocol

    Combination of chemical peels (glycolic, lactic, salicylic), brightening facials, or prescription topicals selected based on skin type and concern.

  3. 3
    Treatment Sessions & Maintenance

    Sessions spaced 2–4 weeks apart; home-care routine provided; maintenance plan to sustain results long-term.

Hair Treatments

What is it?

Female hair thinning is often hormonal. Our approach combines trichoscopy, hormonal profiling, and PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy to diagnose the root cause and stimulate natural regrowth.

Who needs it?

Female Pattern Hair Loss Post-Pregnancy Hair Fall PCOS-Related Thinning Telogen Effluvium Iron Deficiency Hair Loss Alopecia Areata

What to expect

  1. 1
    Trichoscopy & Hormonal Assessment

    Scalp examination with dermatoscopy; blood tests (ferritin, thyroid, androgens, vitamins) to identify the underlying cause.

  2. 2
    Diagnosis & Medical Treatment

    Hormonal correction, nutritional supplementation, topical minoxidil, and oral medications as indicated — tailored to your specific diagnosis.

  3. 3
    PRP Therapy

    Platelet-rich plasma injected into the scalp to stimulate follicles. 3–4 sessions monthly; results visible in 3–6 months.

Laser Procedures

What is it?

Advanced laser treatments for permanent hair reduction, skin rejuvenation, and vaginal wellness — performed by a qualified gynaecologist in a safe, clinical environment.

What can be treated?

Unwanted Hair (Face & Body) Skin Texture & Tone Vaginal Laxity & Dryness Post-Pregnancy Body Changes Scar Reduction

What to expect

  1. 1
    Consultation & Patch Test

    Suitability assessed; skin and hair type mapped; patch test performed 48 hours before the first session.

  2. 2
    Treatment Sessions

    Laser hair reduction requires 6–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Skin rejuvenation or vaginal laser sessions are scheduled per protocol.

  3. 3
    Post-Care & Review

    Sun protection and aftercare instructions provided after each session. Results reviewed at a dedicated follow-up appointment.
