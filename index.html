About the Doctor

Dr. Nupura Gandbhir Borkar

"All about you — your potential, your comfort, your health, and your individuality."

Dr. Nupura Gandbhir Borkar is a specialist Gynaecologist, Obstetrician, and Infertility expert at Healing Hands Clinic in Parel — steps from Tata Memorial Hospital, one of Mumbai's most distinguished medical addresses. She completed her MBBS from D Y Patil Medical College, Navi Mumbai (2004) and her Diploma in Gynaecology & Obstetrics in 2008.

With over 15 years of clinical practice, Dr. Nupura brings a patient-first philosophy to every consultation — listening before treating, and caring for every stage of a woman's health journey from adolescence through menopause.
